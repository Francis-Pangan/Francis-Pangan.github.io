Hi, I'm Francis. I live in Wellington, New Zealand. By day, I work as a membership advisor / admin for Engineering New Zealand, a non-profit organisation helping to engineer a better New Zealand. By night I fight crime as the vigilante el Franko study web development.

I find that Web development is like learning a new language. It's a bit difficult to wrap my head around the technical aspects, and to understand how code works. There's still so much to learn and it can be overwhelming at times! Still, I try to manage my studies and get it to an acceptable, whelming level.

Currently, my focus is on HTML and CSS. It's good that there are heaps of websites out there to help me, because it's bloody confusing sometimes. Took me hours the other day to figure out why something wasn't working, and turns out I was missing a freaking {. Like, I could not even. But when it clicks, it's oh so satisfying. I'm still learning as I go so my website isn't that flash yet, but will be updating as I learn more skills. (Hey pass me some more CSS over here! I thought that was supposed to add style?!)

In my spare time, I like to read fiction adventure books, play video games, but my main hobby is collecting and painting miniatures. Yes, it's a thing. I like to go out and hit the op shops with my wife or just take a walk around Wellington just kidding, who walks?! I jump on Uber scooters! when the weather is nice.
